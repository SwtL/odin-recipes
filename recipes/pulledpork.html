Pulled Pork

Description

Root beer and pulled pork might not seem like a likely pairing but here it makes all the difference. Topped with your favorite BBQ sauce, it's sure to bring rave reviews.

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 1 can or bottle root beer
  • 1 bottle your favorite barbecue sauce
  • 8 burger buns
  1. Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top. Cover and cook on Low until pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours. Note: the actual length of time may vary according to the individual slow cooker.
  2. Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce. Serve on hamburger buns.
